Asian Wonton Salad

ingredients
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
1/2 cup carrot, finely chopped
1/2 cup red pepper, finely chopped

1/2 teaspoon cumin
3/4 cup tomato, chopped

1 15-ounce can black beans, drained (but not rinsed)
1/2 cup fresh orange juice
1 tablespoon orange zest

directions
• Heat the oil in a large skillet or pot over medium high heat.
• Add the garlic, onion, celery, carrot, and red pepper.
• Cook covered for about 5-6 minutes, occasionally stirring.
• Add the cumin and tomatoes.  Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
• Add the beans, orange juice and orange zest.
• Cover and let simmer for 3-5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and the broth has thickened into a nice stew.
