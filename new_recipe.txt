Chocolate Raspberry Tart

ingredients
CRUST
1 cup flour
1 T whole wheat flour
1/3 cup almond flour
10 T (1 1/4 sticks) European butter (cold from freezer)
1/4 cup gin

CARAMEL
1/2 cup cane sugar
2 T heavy cream

RASPBERRY GELEE
1 tsp gelatin
2 cups frozen raspberries
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup cold water
1/2 cup water
1 T pectin

GANACHE
3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
2 T unsalted butter (room temperature) cut into 1/4" squares
4 oz dark chocolate (70%), chopped to bits

instructions
CRUST
• Combine all flours in food processor (pulse to mix)
• Add butter pieces and pulse after each (should look like tiny pebbles)
• Press to 1/2" thick disk

• Wrap in plastic and freeze 20 min

• Thoroughly butter 9" tart pan
• Remove from freezer and soften
• Roll to 1/4" thick circle
• Add to tart pan; press sides into bottom crease; cut to even edge
• Press aluminum foil into dough base, leaving top edge of dough exposed
• Place in freezer 15 min
• Preheat oven to 350
• Bake shell until lightly golden (25-30 min)

CARAMEL
• Spread sugar into heavy saucepan and cook on medium-high heat
• Swirl sugar as it begins to melt (do not stir)
• When dark amber, slowly stir in cream
• Return mixture to a boil, then quickly pour onto baked tart shell
• Refrigerate, then transfer to freezer for 10 min

RASPBERRY GELEE
• Sprinkle gelatin over 1/4 cup water and set aside.
• Bring raspberries, 1/2 cup water and sugar to a boil (medium heat), simmer for 10 min
• Add pectin and mix well
• Strain the raspberry mixture into gelatin-water and stir until completely dissolved
• Pour gelee over caramel tart layer
• Refrigerate until set (5 min)

GANACHE
• Heat cream to simmering in medium saucepan (medium-high heat)
• Place chocolate in bowl and pour half of hot cream over it
• Whisk to mix, then add remaining cream
• Add butter cubes and whisk until fully blended
• Pour chocolate over raspberry layer (tilt to distribute)
• Refrigerate 20 min
• Sprinkle chocolate shavings over tart and serve
