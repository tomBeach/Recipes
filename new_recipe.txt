Corn Pancakes

ingredients
1 cup corn meal
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp sugar
1 cup boiling water
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
2 Tbsp melted butter

1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder

instructions
• blend corn meal, salt, sugar and boiling water
• let stand for 10 minuntes
• beat together the egg, milk and melted butter
• sift together the flour and baking powder
• add to corn meal
• stir flour mix into batter
