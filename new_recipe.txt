Breaded Haddock with Bay Scallops

ingredients
salt
black pepper
1 1/2 pounds haddock
12 bay scallops
4 sprigs fresh thyme (leaves removed, divided)
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 cup sherry
1/4 cup cream
1 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
4 Tbsp butter, divided (2 melted, 2 cubed)

instructions
• Rinse fish and pat dry, then coat with salt & pepper then add to baking dish
• Surround fish with scallops
• Melt 2 Tbsp butter
• Mix melted butter and half of thyme leaves with breadcrumbs
• Sprinkle minced garlic and remaining thyme leaves onto fish/scallops
• Dot remaining 2 Tbsp butter onto fish/scallops
• Mix cream and sherry together and pour over fish/scallops
• Bake at 325 for 20 minutes
• Broil for another 5 minutes, watching CAREFULLY to avoid burning breadcrumbs
